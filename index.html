About Me

Hello! I am Alfa Tijjani Abdulrahman a dedicated researcher with expertise in materials science and chemistry, holding an M.Sc. in Material Science and Explosive Chemistry. My work includes published research on dye sorption, biocoagulant properties, and copolymer preparation. I'm passionate about advancing scientific knowledge to address global challenges, my focus is constantly on innovative solutions in materials and water treatment. Currently I'm Assistant lecturer at Department of chemistry, Federal University of Agriculture, Zuru, Kebbi State, Nigeria. I am actively involved in academic teaching and research. A member of the American Chemical Society and skilled in laboratory analysis, data management, and team collaboration. I have interests in traveling and fostering global connections.

My Future Research Anticipations

  • Advanced Material Science

    Focus areas:

    Reinforced Polymer Materials:

    Extend work on synthesizing and characterizing polymers for bulletproof vests to explore other military and industrial applications, such as lightweight armor and flexible electronics.

    Biocomposites:

    Explore plant-based fibers like Agave Sisalana and Cocos Nucifera for eco-friendly material development.

  • Environmental Chemistry

    Focus Areas:

    Sorption Studies:

    Advance work on dye removal by developing multifunctional adsorbents for industrial wastewater treatment.

    Biocoagulants:

    Further examine the efficiency and scalability of Moringa Oleifera-based systems in water purification.

    Green Chemistry:

    Investigate biodegradable materials for water treatment.

  • Polymer Chemistry and Applications

    Focus Areas:

    Copolymer Engineering: Expand your research on sodium alginate-based copolymers for biomedical applications like drug delivery systems and wound dressings. Smart Polymers: Explore responsive materials for targeted drug delivery or environmental monitoring.
  • Energy and Battery Materials

    Focus Areas:

    Leverage knowledge from moisture determination studies for advancements in battery materials, especially for energy storage in renewable energy systems.

    Investigate novel catalysts for bio-feedstock transformation into sustainable fuels.
